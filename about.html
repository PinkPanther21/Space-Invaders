About Space Invaders

Space Invaders is a classic arcade game that was released in 1978 and has become a symbol of the golden age of video games. Developed by Tomohiro Nishikado and released by Taito, it was one of the first shooting games to gain widespread popularity. Players control a spaceship and must defeat waves of alien invaders that move towards the bottom of the screen.

The game's success led to numerous sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations, making it a cornerstone of retro gaming culture. Its pixelated graphics, iconic sound effects, and addictive gameplay have made it a timeless classic for both old-school and modern gamers.

This tribute website aims to celebrate the nostalgia of Space Invaders, offering players a chance to relive the excitement of the 80s and 90s arcade experience. Prepare for battle and defend the Earth from the alien invasion!
