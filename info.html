Space Invaders Info

About the Game

Space Invaders is a classic arcade game that was first released in 1978. The game was developed by Tomohiro Nishikado and published by Taito. It became one of the most influential video games, laying the groundwork for many future arcade games and helping to establish the video game industry as a whole.

How to Play

In Space Invaders, the player controls a spaceship that can move left or right at the bottom of the screen. The goal is to destroy waves of descending aliens by shooting them with your laser gun. The aliens move across the screen, and as the player destroys them, they gradually increase in speed. The player must avoid getting hit by the aliens or their projectiles.

Game Features

  • Classic 8-bit graphics and sound effects.
  • Increasing difficulty as the game progresses.
  • High-score tracking to compete with friends.
  • Retro arcade experience with modern enhancements.

Legacy of Space Invaders

Space Invaders revolutionized the gaming industry and paved the way for other arcade classics. It was one of the first games to feature continuous gameplay, and its success led to an explosion of arcade games in the following years. Space Invaders is still celebrated today as one of the greatest video games of all time.
